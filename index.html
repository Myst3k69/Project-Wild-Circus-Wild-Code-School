About Us

More than 150 years history

  • 1849

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Oscar Wild, an Illinois resident, a top athlete with a passion for animals, is behind this great story. With his wife, Erika, he decides to put on Lion shows in Chicago. This begins a beautiful and long adventure.

  • 1967

    First show abroad

    More than 100 years after its beginning and 5000 shows across the United States, Wild Circus arrives in Europe for a gigantic tour of more than 500 performances in 50 different cities.

  • December 2012

    The jungle is with us

    More than 500 animals accompany us in all our adventures: lion, white tiger, zebra, giraffe, penguins, hyenas. These animals are part of our family, so we treat them with the utmost respect.

  • 2020

    10 000 shows all over the world

    Wild Circus did it! 10,000 performances across 5 continents! Thank you everybody. Hope this story has no ends.
